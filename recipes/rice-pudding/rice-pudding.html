Rice Pudding

Description

This recipe is from the Boston Cooking-School Book, published in 1898.

Unlike the rice puddings that most Americans know, this rice pudding is baked in an oven, which gives it an exquisite flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups milk
  • ⅓ cup rice
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Grated rind ½ lemon

Steps

  1. Wash rice.
  2. Mix ingredients.
  3. Pour into a buttered dish.
  4. Bake three hours in very slow oven, stirring three times during first hour of baking to prevent rice from settling.
